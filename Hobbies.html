Hobby

A personal hobby that I enjoy is doing Math. Mathcounts is the main way this hobby is expressed. Mathcounts is a math competition that I participated in Maryland where each team is able to select members for the team competition and solo. I enjoy doing this because there is a lot you can learn while you are practicing for the competition and I enjoy the competition and teamwork involved during the day of the competition.
